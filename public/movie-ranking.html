The Best Movies According to Patricia

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

Singing in the Rain

This movies is the pinicle of the golden age of musicals. It has big memorable song and dance numbers and enough comedy to keep you entertained. It is a true classic and a must watch at least once for any movie buff.

The Breakfast Club

The characters of this movie are cliches in the most charming way. Growing up in the 80's this story highlights the social separation that dominated high school life but brings a spark of hope for understanding.

Ladyhawke

Just a good movie.

Top 3 Superhero Movies

Iron Man

An actor I like and a comic hero I knew nothing about made for a perfect match.

Gardians of the Galaxy

What can I say but fun.

Batman (1989)

Dare I say that Michael Keaton was the best of the buch when it comes to the list of actor that have portrayed this iconic superhero? Pair him with the villony of Jack Nicholson and you get one good movie.

Top 3 Movies I Didn't think I Would Like But Did

Dirty Dancing

I was forced to go see this movie with no hint of what it was about. The film easily sucks the viewer in with it's musice and dancing that supports the drama of the main story.

Doctor Strange

Marvel Comic's Doctor Strange is not a character I was ever familiar with. However, hearing that the Doctor would be a key figure other movies in the Marvel franchise, I decided I needed to watch it. I was surprised by the comedy and acting skills of the cast.

Flatliners

I don't like horror movies, but this one is much more of a thriller. The cast is great and the story compelling enough to make me jump out of my seat without the overwhelming gore so popular today.
